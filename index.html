Umaru Doma

Umaru is a perfect sister in public but when she gets home... She becomes the laziest sister!!

In Himouto! Umaru-chan, Umaru is a high school student living with her older brother. She has perfect grades, athletically talented, and is very beautiful. She helps elderly and helps anyone in trouble. However when she gets home she becomes himouto (a pun on imoto, which means little sister, and himono, which means dried fish). She dones her cape and eats snacks, plays games, and takes naps all day everyday!
